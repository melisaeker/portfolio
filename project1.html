VERTİGO GAMES POLYGUN ARENA UI-UX DESİGNER JUNIOR 2D DEMO

In the final UI design, the goal was to preserve the brand identity by staying true to the game’s existing color palette. To strengthen the visual language, subtle adjustments were made to tone and lighting values, while the layout of elements was refined to improve accessibility and enhance the user experience. Since a suitable PNG asset for the background was not available, it was generated using AI and integrated into the interface. Buttons and UI variants were manually designed to accurately reflect different interaction states.

The player name is positioned at the top center of the screen, aligning with the general layout conventions used in Vertigo Games titles. Maintaining this consistency helps preserve the brand’s visual language and increases user familiarity.The online status is also placed in the top center area to ensure quick readability.Settings and notification icons are positioned in the top-right corner to provide ergonomic access. The layout is inspired by the Zooba interface. Level information is presented in the top-left corner, a common placement in mobile games, allowing players to track their progress instantly.The shop and inventory are grouped vertically on the left side to ensure structured navigation and reduce cognitive load. Grouping progression and equipment-related features in one area helps simplify user interaction.The XP bar is placed in the bottom-left corner to provide continuous feedback without dominating the screen. To make XP tracking easier, it is positioned alongside missions (inspired by Brawl Stars).The Play button is clearly distinguished as the primary call-to-action, ensuring high visibility and separation from other interface elements.

DESIGN APPROACH

  • Familiar layouts were used to reduce the learning curve
  • Reference games were analyzed for usability and ergonomics
  • Visual balance between primary and secondary elements was established
  • The structure was designed to be scalable for future features
